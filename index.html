Lalisa Manoban(Member of Blackpink)


Lalisa Manoban is a Thai rapper, singer, dancer, and model. She is based in South Korea and goes by the stage name Lisa. She is known for being a member of Blackpink, which as of 2019 is the highest-charting female k-pop group. She is also active as a fashion model and has appeared solo on the cover of Harper's Bazaar in Thailand. Lisa is also well known on social media. She is the most followed k-pop singer on Instagram, with 49 million followers. Lisa was born in Thailand on 27 March 1997. In 2010, she auditioned in Thailand with YG Entertainment. She was invited to join them and was eventually added to Blackpink. Her solo songs are Lalisa and Money. She also collabed with DJ Snake, Ozuna and Megan Thee Stallion in their song, SG.
